Our Mission & Philosophy

Why we exist and what drives us forward

Transforming Academic Excellence into Real-World Impact

The Youth-Innovation Lab #19 was founded on a simple but powerful principle: your top students become your innovators. We believe that academic excellence should translate into tangible solutions for society's most pressing problems.

For too long, the brightest minds in our educational institutions have been siloed in theoretical learning. Lab #19 bridges the gap between academic knowledge and practical application by providing a structured environment where students can apply their learning to real-world challenges.

Our lab operates on three core pillars: Identification of exceptional talent, Incubation of innovative ideas, and Implementation of viable solutions.

Our Journey

How Lab #19 evolved from an idea to a leading innovation hub

Lab Founded

Youth-Innovation Lab #19 was established with 12 student innovators and 3 faculty mentors. Our first location was a converted classroom with basic equipment.

2018

First Major Success

Our water purification project won the National Youth Innovation Award, securing our first major grant and attracting attention from industry partners.

2019

Expansion & Growth

Moved to our current 5,000 sq ft facility with state-of-the-art equipment. Expanded our program to include 40 student innovators across multiple disciplines.

2020

Industry Partnerships

Formalized partnerships with 8 major tech companies who now provide mentorship, resources, and internship opportunities for our students.

2021

Current Impact

To date: 150+ student innovators trained, 50+ projects completed, 15 projects funded for further development, 3 becoming successful social enterprises.

2023
YouthInnovationLab

Transforming top students into innovators who build solutions for society's most pressing challenges. Lab #19 is leading the way in youth-driven innovation.
